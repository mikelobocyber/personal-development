# Fitness

Strength, endurance, and body composition. Built around a home gym with power rack, barbell, adjustable dumbbells, pulley system, dip bars, and treadmill.

## Goals

- Build strength and maintain muscle through a cut
- Run consistently alongside lifting
- Hit body composition targets through controlled nutrition

## Principles

- 2 RIR (reps in reserve) — never to failure
- Compounds: 2-3 min rest
- Accessories: 60-90 sec rest
- Sleep: non-negotiable at 8+ hrs

## Files

- `training-plan.md` — current weekly split and exercise selection
- `nutrition.md` — calorie targets, macros, and food sources
